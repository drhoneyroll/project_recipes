Steak rolls

Description

Steak rolls are a famous Serbian dish, made easily with steak meat and pie crust!

Ingredients

  • A piece of steak
  • Pie crusts
  • 1 tbl spoon of pumpking butter
  • 0.5l of oil
  • 1 tbl spoon of sour cream
  • pumpkin seeds to garnish
  • 1 tea spoon of curry spice

How to make

  1. Cut the steak into very small cubes. Season the meat with some salt, mixed pepper and curry spice.
  2. Roll the meat into the crusts to form a small tube filled with meat.
  3. Make a sauce by mixing sour cream and pumpkin butter. Add some salt to taste. Cook the sauce over low heat, very short. Don't allow the sauce to boil!
  4. Fry the steak rolls in oil until the crusts are golden.
  5. Serve the rolls with sauce on top of them and garnish with pumpkin seeds.
